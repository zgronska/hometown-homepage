Visit Haarlem

Like Amsterdam, but smaller, and without the annoying tourists and tourist traps.

Top three activities to do in Haarlem

Shop at the Grote Markt

The Grote Markt is a large square in the centre of Haarlem, surrounded by countless restaurants, cafes, and stores, as well as the 1000 year old St. Bavokerk. Whether you're up for shopping, lunch, or a party, you'll find it all here!

Explore the dunes

Just outside the town is the Zuid-Kennemerland National Park, where you can take a walk through rugged dunes, with a varied mosaic of forest, to the youngest dunes along the North Sea coast. If you're lucky, you'll meet deer, wild horses, fluffy cows, or even a herd of bison!

Take a bike ride

The primary mode of transportation in the Netherlands is the bicycle, so hop on and take a ride around the town and beyond! Cycling not your thing? We have boats too.
